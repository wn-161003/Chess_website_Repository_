Chess - Openings

Why is learning openings important?

Learning chess openings is important as it helps you safely develop your pieces, control the board and protect your king. This prevents early losses and memorising these moves helps prevent quick and cheap mates. It also helps you stop losing material to common opening traps.

Sicilian Defense

The Sicilian Defense is one of the more popular opeings, commonly taught to begginers because of its ease of execution.

Italian Game

The Italian Game is a way for players to rapidly develop onto the board

Queen's Gambit

The Queen's Gambit is centred around long term advantages with positioning
